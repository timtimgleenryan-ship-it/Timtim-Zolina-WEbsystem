Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Prep: 15 min Cook: 15 min Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • to taste black pepper
  • to taste salt

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Bring a large pot of salted water to boil and cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.

  2. 2.

    While the pasta is cooking, fry the pancetta in a large pan over medium heat until crispy. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant, then remove from heat.

  3. 3.

    In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated cheeses, and a generous amount of black pepper.

  4. 4.

    When the pasta is done, reserve a cup of the pasta water, then drain the pasta and add it to the pan with the pancetta.

  5. 5.

    Remove the pan from heat and quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, tossing continuously. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce.

  6. 6.

    Season with salt and additional pepper to taste. Serve immediately with extra grated cheese on top.

Nutrition Information

Per serving (approximate):

  • Calories: 750kcal
  • Carbohydrates: 75g
  • Protein: 32g
  • Fat: 32g
  • Saturated Fat: 12g
